contribute

darlingdance is committed to creating high quality works with superb collaborators in Washington, DC, and beyond, and we can't do it without your help. If you're interested in supporting our current work, please consider contributing at the button below. Your support could help costume our dancers, design our lighting, or produce our sound, and we would be honored to have you join those processes through your contributions. Supporters will be listed on our website, and acknowledged in future program materials. If you have any questions about supporting darlingdance, please email us at darlingdanceco@gmail.com. Thank you!

darlingdance is a sponsored project of Fractured Atlas, a non-profit arts service organization. Fractured Atlas will receive grants for the charitable purposes of darlingdance, provide oversight to ensure that grant funds are used in accordance with grant agreements, and provide reports as required by the grantor. Contributions for the charitable purposes of darlingdance must be made payable to Fractured Atlas and are tax deductible to the extent permitted by law.
